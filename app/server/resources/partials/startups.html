Register to host a course. Hosting is a great way to get your name out amongst developers, and meet curious and talented developers.

What does "hosting" a course mean?

Hosting a course means you have Wifi and can seat eleven people, for one evening a week for four weeks (the typical length of a course). Hosts are listed as sponsors for the course, and you're free to mingle & advertise yourselves to the developers in attendance.

Which course will I host?

We'll ask you to host whichever one of our courses is starting at the same time as you have availablity to host. We try to match you with courses that emphasise a technology you use.

What if we'd like to teach a course based on our in-house tech?

If your company has an interesting stack, or has developed some really interesting technology, we'd love to talk about co-designing a course with you.

Who attends these courses?

Our students are experienced developers that are interested in exploring new technologies.
